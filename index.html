War Card Game

War is a classic two-player card game played with the standard 52-card deck. Our product retains everything you love about the card game while putting our unique twist on it.

Once you load the game in your browser, you will be sent to the Start menu. Here, you can read thorough instructions on how to play, play the game, or check out more information about the developers.

Once you click start, the dealer will shuffle the 52-card deck and split it down the middle (26 for you and 26 for your opponent). Now, all you have to do is click "DEAL" to play one card for you and one card for your opponent.

The rules are simple:

1. Only VALUE matters (Ace is the lowest and King is the highest)

2. Highest card wins

3. If bother player's value is the SAME, WAR is Triggered

4. In WAR, both players play 4 cards (3 face down, 1 face UP)

5. Highest card wins and winner takes all cards on the table

6. The first player to have more than 26 cards wins the game!
